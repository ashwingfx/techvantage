ABOUT Cdocz

Intelligent Document Processing improves your productivity by enhancing your documentation process efficiency, be it migrating and archiving policies, scrutinizing claims or validating KYC in insurance, extracting data from medical invoices, digitalizing prescriptions in healthcare, improving customer experience through automated forms and underwriting in BFSI or reading vehicle number plates from images.

Intelligent Document Processing moving beyond traditional OCR

Traditional legacy Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and Intelligent Character Recognition (ICR) tools transcribes images to texts with no room for classification or information extraction. Cdocz is an advanced Intelligent Document Processor that takes several steps further, categorizes, classifies and exports relevant data for further processing using AI technologies. Cdocz can automatically pinpoint important information and extract it for further analysis from complex structured/unstructured data with better-than-human performance.
